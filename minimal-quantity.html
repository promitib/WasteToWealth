Introducing: The Smart Bin

Smart Bin is an AI-powered system that automatically sorts waste into recyclable, organic, and hazardous categories to promote eco-friendly disposal and efficient recycling.

  • Automated Waste Sorting: Uses AI and sensors to detect and categorize waste into recyclable, organic, and hazardous types.
  • Environmental Impact: Promotes eco-friendly disposal, reduces landfill waste, and enhances recycling efficiency.
  • Target Usage: Designed for smart cities, institutions, and sustainable waste management systems
